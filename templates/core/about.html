Our History

Founded in 2010, Yeab Pharmaceuticals One Member PLC began as a vision to bridge the gap between quality medical products and healthcare facilities in need.

Over the years, we have grown from a small import business to a comprehensive healthcare solutions provider, establishing partnerships with leading manufacturers worldwide and building a reputation for reliability and excellence.

Today, we serve over 50 healthcare facilities across the region, importing and distributing more than 500 different pharmaceutical products and medical equipment items.
