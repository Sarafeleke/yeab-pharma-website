Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on product availability and location. Typically, standard orders take 3-7 business days, while urgent orders can be delivered within 24-48 hours.

Do you provide technical support for equipment?

Yes, we provide comprehensive technical support, installation, and training for all medical equipment we supply.

Are your products internationally certified?

All our products meet international quality standards and have necessary certifications from relevant health authorities.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile payments, and other secure payment methods. Payment terms can be discussed based on order volume and relationship.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer competitive pricing and bulk discounts for large orders. Contact our sales team for customized pricing based on your requirements.

How do you ensure product quality?

We work directly with reputable manufacturers, conduct quality inspections, and maintain proper storage conditions to ensure product integrity.
